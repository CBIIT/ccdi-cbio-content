## Pediatric solid tumor PDXs (UTHSA - UT Health San Antonio, 2023)
The pediatric solid tumor PDX data was doanlowed directly from the [Peds cBioPortal](https://pedcbioportal.kidsfirstdrc.org/datasets) instance. To learn more about how the dataset was prepared, please refer to this [publication](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37990009/).
